About SNHPI

We combine hands-on practice with modern pedagogy to develop confident, industry-ready diploma engineers. Our goal — technology-driven, inclusive education.

Shamshun Nahar Harun Polytechnic Institute (SNHPI) was established with a clear mission — to equip students with real-world skills through practical learning and a project-based curriculum. Our dedicated faculty, well-equipped laboratories, and strong academic support help students build the confidence and competence needed for their professional growth.

We are deeply committed to providing an education that blends theory with real industry practice. Through active partnerships with local industries, our students gain hands-on experience in internships, real-life projects, and placement opportunities — preparing them to understand how professional engineering teams work and to step into their careers with confidence.

Our Approach

  • Project-based Learning: Teamwork and presentations focused on solving real-world problems.
  • Industry Mentorship: Guidance from both faculty and an expert industry panel.
  • Modern Labs: Fully equipped and updated laboratories for Electrical, CMT, Civil, Mechanical, Auto, and Textile departments.
  • Career Support: CV clinics, mock interviews, job boards, and a strong alumni network.

Milestones & Growth

Labs & Studios

Spinning Lab

  • Blow room, carding, drawing demo
  • Ring/compact spinning overview
  • Yarn count & twist tests

Weaving & Knitting

  • Loom setup (warp/weft), knitting machines
  • Fabric structure & defects
  • CAD patterns (intro)

Dyeing & Finishing

  • Lab-dip, shade matching
  • Printing & finishing trials
  • QA tests (GSM, pilling, fastness)

Career Paths

Spinning Operator / Junior Technologist

Machine setup, count/TPM, quality logs, shift reporting।

Weaving/Knitting Technician

Loom/knit machine setting, faults & efficiency tracking।

Dyeing & Finishing Assistant

Recipe, lab-dip, batch supervision, shade approval process।

QA/Testing Assistant

GSM, tensile, pilling, color fastness, inline/FP audit support।

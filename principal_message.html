Assalamu Alaikum. Welcome to Shamshun Nahar Harun Polytechnic Institute (SNHPI). At SNHPI, we believe that learning is more than completing a syllabus — it’s about hands-on projects, teamwork, and the practice of ethics. Our mission is to help every student develop employable skills, a problem-solving mindset, and strong character.

Our curriculum thoughtfully blends theory with intensive lab work. Faculty members collaborate closely with industry mentors to continually refine course outcomes, ensuring students remain aligned with real-world standards. Through structured internship and placement support, our graduates are well-prepared to transition confidently into professional engineering roles.

We foster an inclusive learning environment where every student can grow at their own pace. For beginners, we provide foundational support; for advanced learners, we offer specialized project tracks — maintaining a balance that encourages both personal and professional growth.

“Learn by building. Lead with integrity.” — This is our north star.

Finally, I invite you to visit our labs, talk to our students, and experience the culture first-hand. Together, let’s build a future where skills meet purpose.

Modern Labs

Electrical, CMT, Civil, Mechanical, Auto, Textile—updated equipment & safety-first SOPs.

Mentored Learning

Project studios, design reviews, and peer critique for deeper understanding.

Career Support

CV clinic, mock interviews, internship pipelines, and alumni network.
